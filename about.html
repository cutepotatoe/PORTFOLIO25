About Me

Hi

I’m Ana Beatriz, a graphic designer based in Madrid, Spain

You can call me Ana or Bea, or both if you wish ;) Design and overall the world of graphic arts, music, cinema etc. has always caught my eye. Wether it be drawing, making clothes for my dolls, fashion or make up; design, branding and art were always what I've loved to do, explore and think about in my free time.

Seeing through the eye of empathic and reflective apreciation for the visual arts is my way of living. 'Cause no matter how many hobbies I try, how many times you changes styles, the vision and spirit remains intact.

To me, that's what makes me a designer. Not the trends, not the styles but the love for what works and causes wonders to the eye, soul.

To appreciate, to inspire, to change.

Long story short, I care about the soul of things. I think a lot, perhaps overthink. But be sure if it is like so, it's because I actually care.

Want to know me better? Well then you'll have to see it for yourself. Hit me up. The world can always be better, or at least more fun. Dearing perhaps?
